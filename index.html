A LEADING FRANCHISE CONSULTANT

John L. Rogers is internationally recognized in franchise law, and since July 1, 2020, has moved from law to consulting. John has been recommended by L'expert many times, he has been named to the International Who’s Who of Franchise Lawyers and designated a “Legal Eagle” in the U.S. by Franchise Times. John has decades of experience helping franchisors, franchisees and their advisors navigate the legal complexities of franchising.

John has been admitted to the bar in both British Columbia and Ontario and has experience advising on franchise legislation and drafting of franchise disclosure documents in all franchise-regulated provinces. For four years, John served as general counsel, a director and secretary of the Canadian Franchise Association. In those roles he helped develop policies and programs for the betterment of the franchise industry in Canada and promotion of ethical conduct and camaraderie among industry members.

Franchising is about faithfully duplicating the factors responsible for a franchisor's success.
Faithful duplication of every aspect of a business model, operating systems, products, training and marketing is what makes visiting your favourite coffee spot in Vancouver or Dallas feel the same. It’s what makes the hamburger you had in Beijing taste just like the one back home. And, it’s what makes the franchise business model work. At Likewise, we review or recommend franchise documents to ensure faithful duplication and engender respect for a business relationship by clearly defining the roles and responsibilities of all parties to franchise agreements including franchisors, franchisees and suppliers.
Have you found the secret sauce?

Franchisors

If you have a concept and are considering starting or expanding a franchise system in Canada, we can help. Likewise advises franchisors on:

  • Agent registration of Canadian trade marks.
  • Strategic advice on franchise agreements and disclosure documents.
  • Agent incorporation of franchisors.
  • Advising on leases/subleases.
  • General franchise advice.

Franchisees

For prospective franchisees, our services include:

  • Review of franchise disclosure documents and franchise agreements.
  • Memos summarizing our reviews and providing our recommendations for changes.
  • Agent incorporation of franchisees.
  • Leasing/subleasing advice.
  • General advice on becoming a franchisee.
Expanding to Canada?

U.S. Franchisors

Are you a U.S. franchisor considering an expansion to Canada? For U.S. franchisors, we provide:

  • Advice on ’Canadianization’ of franchise, master franchise and area development agreements.
  • Advice on Canadian franchise disclosure documents.
  • Agent registration of Canadian trade marks.
  • Agent incorporation of Canadian subsidiaries.
  • General advice on compliance with Canadian franchise practices.

Up Front Fees

We appreciate that many clients dislike hourly rates because they want to know, up front, what their fees are going to be. That’s why Likewise offers the option of Up Front Pricing.

Once major matters required of us have been determined, we will provide an up front fee quote in a client engagement letter, with a retainer required. The up front fee will apply to all work specified in the engagement letter. Only if additional work is required will an hourly rate apply. Disbursements and taxes will be extra. The engagement letter will cover all of these points.

No duplication of fees.

A licensed lawyer will finalize all agreements. We ensure that the combination of consulting fees and legal fees for completing any transaction will not exceed what John would have charged as a lawyer.
